Know Yourself

Low risk — Bonds, savings, stable ETFs
Medium — Mix of stocks & bonds (ETFs)
High risk — Individual stocks, crypto

Your answer shapes your entire strategy. No wrong answer — just be honest.

Know the Difference

Stocks vs. Crypto

What's actually backing your investment?

🏢

Stocks / ETFs

  • Real companies with revenue
  • Employees, assets, products
  • Regulated by governments
  • ~100+ years of track record
  • Your foundation
vs
🪙

Crypto

  • Not backed by company assets
  • Highly speculative
  • Less regulated
  • Extreme volatility
  • Optional & riskier

Crypto isn't bad — but stocks/ETFs are the foundation. Build there first.
